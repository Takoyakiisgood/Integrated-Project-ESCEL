INSTRUCTIONS

  • Players will select the mode of gameplay they want to play, which is either PVP (Player versus player) or Adventure.
  • PVP mode allows the player to challenge another player of their skill level.
  • Adventure mode allows the player to fight monsters to get pass a certain level.
  • All players start of with 100 health points.
  • Players are to type the sentence that is displayed on the screen and each error would cause the player's health points to drop at a certain amount.
  • When the player types 3/4 of the sentence wrong, the player would lose 20 health points.

PURPOSE

Our purpose is to help the younger generation type fast with precision. In our digital age, more and more employees would want to hire candidates that are skilled in typing with speed and precision for increased productivity and efficiency.
With more and more specialisations that require the skill of typing fast, this website application feature aims to help people who has little to no experience typing on a computer keyboard,
mainly the younger generation, hone their typing skills for their future.
You can also compete with many other Warriors in the world by ranking up on the leaderboards and by defeating more monsters and evolving more pets!
