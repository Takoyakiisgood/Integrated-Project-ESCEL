INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome aboard fellow Keyboard Warrior! The land of Animalia has been taken over by evil Robot Monsters, these scheming monsters have stolen many pet eggs from Animalia... Be that hero that Animalia needs and rescue it from the demonic hands of the Robot Monsters!

  • Select Adventure mode from the "Play" tab on the navigation bar to play.
  • In Adventure mode, there are 3 rounds of typing you need to clear in order to defeat the monster.
  • The difficulty of the sentence generated would be based on your level. (Easy: level 15 and below, Medium: level 30 and below, Hard: above level 30)
    NOTE: sentence generated for guests would be of difficulty easy, so be sure to register for an account if you want to challenge yourself!
  • You will start off with 100 HP and HP would be deducted throughout the 3 rounds if typing errors are made. (1 error = -1 HP)
  • When your HP falls to 0, you lose! But fret not, you won't lose anything other than not being able to gain EXP/Eggs.
  • At the end of each round you'll be alerted of your Words Per Minute(WPM). So if you're a competitive try to top the leaderboard with the fastest WPM!
  • When you defeat a Monster, the monster has a 20% chance of dropping a pet egg. Pet eggs are then added into your inventory under the "Home" tab, you can grow the eggs by clicking on the "Grow!" button which would appear when you hover over an egg. To fully evolve a pet, simply play 5 games after clicking the "Grow!" button. Fully evolved pets can be stored and viewed in your inventory.
  • When you defeat a Monster, you gain 5 EXP. At level 1, you will need 15 EXP to level up, and an additional 5 EXP each time you level up. (E.g. level 2: 20 EXP, level 3: 25 EXP etc.)
  • Once you have registered with us, log in and you'll be able to select a default profile picture of your choice!

PURPOSE

Our purpose is to help the younger generation type fast with precision. In our digital age, more and more employees would want to hire candidates that are skilled in typing with speed and precision for increased productivity and efficiency. With more and more specialisations that require the skill of typing fast, this website application feature aims to help people who has little to no experience typing on a computer keyboard, mainly the younger generation, to hone their typing skills for the future. Players can also compete with many other Warriors in the world by ranking up on the leaderboards and by defeating more monsters and evolving more pets!

CHALLENGE YOURSELF!

You can compete against many other Warriors in the world by just typing! Easy, right? All you need to do is to sign up with us and start typing!

DEFEATING MONSTERS

Not only can you defeat cool monsters, you can also keep adorable pets! Monsters have a 20% chance of dropping eggs that you can grow! Compete with other Warriors including your friends to see who can collect more pets!
WORDS PER MINUTE
